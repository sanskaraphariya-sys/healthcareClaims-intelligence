## Vision

The Healthcare Claims Intelligence Platform will be developed incrementally, beginning with a strong business foundation before progressing through data engineering, analytics, backend development, artificial intelligence, and cloud deployment.

Each phase builds upon the previous one while maintaining alignment with the overall product vision.
The Healthcare Claims Intelligence Platform is an operational decision-support system designed for Clinical Investigators.

The platform improves the quality, speed, explainability, and consistency of healthcare claims investigations.

Rather than replacing investigators or adjudicating claims, the platform centralizes evidence, organizes clinical knowledge, provides AI-assisted recommendations, and captures structured review data that can be analyzed to continuously improve investigation performance.
